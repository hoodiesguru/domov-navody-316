Geotargeting Strategies for Global Networks

Publikoval František Procházka | 17. 07. 2026 | ⏰ 1 min čtení

Local relevance is crucial. When deploying a network of resources, attaching specific geographic metadata (such as ICBM coordinates and Dublin Core schemas) assists crawlers contextualize the resource.

By switching local business schemas across sites, entities can simulate organic geographic distribution without running physical infrastructure in those areas.
